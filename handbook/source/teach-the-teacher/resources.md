# Additional Resources

A list of open, community-driven resources for instructors and students.

## Open-Source Tools
- [YosysHQ](https://yosyshq.net/yosys/) — Logic synthesis tool.  
- [OpenROAD Project](https://openroad.readthedocs.io/) — Physical implementation.  
- [LibreLane](https://gitlab.com/librelane) — Integrated open PDK-based flow.  
- [Magic VLSI](http://opencircuitdesign.com/magic/) — Layout and DRC.  
- [KLayout](https://www.klayout.de/) — Layout viewer and LVS.  
- [Ngspice](https://ngspice.sourceforge.io/) — Analog simulation.  

## Educational Initiatives
- [VSD Open-Source Workshops](https://www.vlsisystemdesign.com/)  
- [IEEE CASS UNIC Program](https://ieee-cas.org/universalization-ic-design-cass-unic-cass)  
- [ChipUSM CANELOS Workshop](https://chipusm.github.io/)  
- [Open-Source Silicon Initiative (FOSSi)](https://fossi-foundation.org/)  

## Recommended References
- Jespers, Paul & Murmann, Boris. *Systematic Design of Analog CMOS Circuits.*  
- Kayal, Maher. *Structured Analog CMOS Design.*  
- [ASIC World](https://asic-world.com/) — Tutorials on digital design and verification.
